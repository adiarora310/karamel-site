The metric

True Reach counts the people who actually show up.

Your owned audience plus the people who engage with you both ways. Not the follower number, the number that converts.

True Reach = the audience you own (email, subscribers, direct contacts) + the people who reply, share, and message you back. Everyone else is just a count.

Metric visual: 40,000 followers narrowing to 2,300 True Reach (owned audience plus two-way engagers)

True Reach vs vanity metrics

  • What it counts

    Vanity: every follower, including bots, ghosts, and people who scrolled past once.

    True Reach: the audience you own plus the people who reply, share, and DM.

  • Which direction it moves

    Vanity: one way. They see you, you never hear back.

    True Reach: two ways. You are actually in a conversation.

  • What it predicts

    Vanity: a bigger number on a dashboard.

    True Reach: the relationships and revenue that follow from them.
